Often cited as one of their strongest releases, Ween's Chocolate and Cheese marked the return to the full band forumla, having spent the previous two albums working and touring with the DAT drum machine. Chocolate and Cheese has a heavy American vibe throughout, brandishing it's genius only between flashes of excess and obscenity!
