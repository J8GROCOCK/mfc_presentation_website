Inarguably the brownest of all ween releases, The Pod was recorded in a barn on a stable when the brothers Ween turned 19! Featuring what would become classics in their cannon such as The Stallion parts one and two and Captain Fantasy The Pod sees ween take on a more narrow scope than their previous album, dropping the dumkit in favour of a drum machine for a more modern and lo-fi aesthetic. Now you know who you're messing with, Mangs.
