Written and Recorded in the wake of Gener's divorce, Quebec marks a maturing of Weens sound. With the same world class production standards as the previous release White Pepper, but more off the wall wacky compositional choices - Quebec brings together the best of the two disparate worlds of ween - Off the wall lo-fi energy meets measured high end song craft!
