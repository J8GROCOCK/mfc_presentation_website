Ween produced official studio records from 1990 begining with God Ween Satan : The Oneness, a greatest hits of sorts, chronicling the best of the music greated by Gene and Dean ween between the bands formation in 1984 until their shared eighteen birthdays in 1990.

From releasing on their own label ShimmyDisc to eventually signing with Elektra Records for the release of 1992's Pure Guava, Ween have maintained their distinct edge and ability to showcase their humour, songwriting talent and relentless pursuit of the odd regrdless of circumstance.
