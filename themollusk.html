Weens most Weenie release, the Mollusk was an incredible conceptual hit for the brothers Ween! A Nautical themed album recorded at a beach house in new hope, The Mollusk captures in it's sound the essence of the sea. The opening track I'm Dancing in the Show Tonight via the modulated voice of Gener created Spongebob and Patrick Star's voices and the second last track Ocean Man closed out the 2004 Spongebob Movie!
