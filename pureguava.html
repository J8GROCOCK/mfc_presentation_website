The final album of the "Brown Trilogy", Ween's Pure Guava marks the begining of the bands ascent to international noteriety. Having developed a large local following in New Hope the band's third record (while recorded in tandem with The Pod) was picked up by major label Elektraand distributed globally. This album saw the release of Push th' Little Daisies, which would chart in the Australian Top Ten, giving Ween their only top ten hit to date! Lo-fi sounds and scattered production make this album sound like the conclusion to a closing chapter rather than the beginning of Ween's rise to global prominence.
